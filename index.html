I am a PhD student at EPFL, advised by Prof. Michael Gastpar.

My research focuses on the theoretical foundations and practical methods for building reliable, trustworthy, and efficient machine learning systems, with an emphasis on uncertainty quantification and robust decision-making.

  • Inference-time adaptation for large language models (LLMs)
  • Uncertainty quantification for machine learning systems, including conformal prediction
  • Preference learning and LLM alignment
  • Finite-sample and non-asymptotic analysis of learning algorithms
